Engineering Scholarship in India NSEE

National Scholarship Entrance Examination (NSEE) is a national level merit-based scholarship exam that provides the scholarship for medical and engineering aspirants in India. Through its Scholarship program, NSEE has fueled dreams of more than 2 lakh talented engineering and medical students of India since its initiation. NSEE started with baby feet at 2020 with 200 students enrolling for engineering and medical scholarships and by 2020, it has grown to be a national-level merit-exam providing more than 37K engineering scholarships and medical scholarships all over India.

More about NSEE

NSEE is an Eligibility test for obtaining scholarships in engineering and medical scholarships. The exam conducting board is registered with the Ministry of Finance act 1994 under the central excise department of India. The objective of the NSEE Scholarship program is to provide financial assistance to the talented, meritorious engineering and medical students through its scholarship exam that is conducted all over india.

NSEE has launched two scholarship programs-

  • NSEE 2021 Scholarship for engineering and medical aspirants.
  • NSEE-2021 Open Book Scholarship for medical and engineering aspirants.

The NSEE Scholarship exam is usually conducted every year in the month of April and May. The Scholarship exam will be conducted all across India and the examination will be conducted in Online mode (Computer Based Test) only. Based on the candidate score in the NSEE exam and as per the layout of NSEE scholarship, the scholarship will be funded to the respective students.

Scholarship for everyone
Eligibility for NSEE Scholarship:
  • Class 12th passed/appearing students from any recognized board throughout India will be eligible for the NSEE scholarship.
  • Candidates who have passed class 12th in 2016, 2017 and 2020 or appearing class 12th exam in 2021 will be eligible for NSEE 2021 examination.
  • First-year students who have got admission into engineering or medical can also apply for NSEE to avail scholarships for engineering or medical scholarships.
Note: The scholarships is applicable for Engineering and MBBS/BDS studies only.

AWARDS AND BENEFITS OF NSEE SCHOLARSHIP

The eligible students will be awarded with the scholarships and rewards as per their performance in NSEE examination and according to the NSEE Scholarship slab.

  • NSEE Scholarships upto 100% for Engineering and Medical students of Government college.
  • NSEE Scholarships upto Rs.80,000/- for Engineering and Medical students of Private college.

Awards of NSEE Scholarship for Engineering:

  • Students .ing 85-100% marks in NSEE are awarded Engineering Scholarship up to 4 years.
  • Students .ing 50-65% marks in NSEE get Engineering Scholarship up to 1 year.
  • Students .ing 45-50% marks get rewards such as laptops/tabs from NSEE.

Awards of NSEE Scholarship for Medical:

  • Students clearing NSEE Exam with 85-100% score are awarded Medical Scholarship up to 5 years.
  • Students .ing 50-65% marks in NSEE get Medical Scholarship from NSEE up to 1 year.
  • Students .ing 45-50% marks are rewarded with study-related electronic gadgets such as laptops/tabs from NSEE.

How to Apply for NSEE Scholarship?

Follow the below steps to apply for the scholarship:

Step 1: Visit the official registration page of NSEE to apply for engineering and medical scholarships in India.

Step 2: Fill out all the details asked such as Name, DOB, Email id, Phone number and click on ‘Click to Register’ button. A confirmation SMS/email containing registration number and password will be sent to the registered number/email id.

Step 3: Using the received registered number and password,Login to proceed with the further registration process. Choose the application type as NSEE-2021 Application or NSEE-2021 Open Book Application and proceed further.

Step 4: Fill out all the details such as your communication details, and select four preferred exam centers as per convenience.

Step 5: Pay the application fee through the online mode of payment such as debit card, credit card or net banking.

Step 6: Enter the academic details, guardian/parent details, Annual Family Income and click on ‘Click to Proceed’ button.

Step 7: Finally, Upload the scanned copy of recent passport sized photograph and signature and click on ‘Click here to Proceed’ button. The images should be in png/jpeg/jpg format only. The file size of the scanned copy of photograph should not exceed 100kb and the file size of the signature should not exceed 50kb.

You will be redirected to the Confirmation Page, where you can check all your details filled along with Application Status. Download the confirmation page and keep it for future reference.

Note: Applicants are advised to take the print out of acknowledgement page for further reference.

NSEE Scholarship EXAM PATTERN

NSEE Conduct two type of scholarship exam in India - NSEE Scholarship Exam and NSEE Open-book Scholarship Exam.

The exam will be computer-based and the pattern of paper will be Multiple Choice Questions. The questions will be asked from class 12th Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/Biology (based on the course selected).

Exam Pattern for NSEE Scholarship Exam:
Duration of Examination

90 Minutes
Total Marks

360 marks.
Question Type

Multiple Choice Question
Number of Questions

90 questions and each subject, i.e Physics, Chemistry, Maths/Biology carrying 30 Questions.

Marking Scheme

4 mark will be awarded for every correct answer. There is negative marking for wrong answers and for every wrong answer, 1 mark will be deducted. No marks will be given for unattempted questions.

Exam Pattern for NSEE Open Book Scholarship:
Duration of Examination

90 Minutes
Total Marks

480 marks.
Question Type

Multiple Choice Question
Number of Questions

120 questions and each subject, i.e Physics, Chemistry, Maths/Biology carrying 40 Questions.

Marking Scheme

4 mark will be awarded for every correct answer. There is negative marking for wrong answers and for every wrong answer, 1 mark will be deducted. No marks will be given for unattempted questions.
