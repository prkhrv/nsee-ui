Scholarship for Medical Students - AISEE

AISEE stands for All India Scholarship Entrance Examinations which conducts national level scholarship examination which is registered with the department of Ministry of finance under the act 1994 and registered with the department of India central excise.

When the scholarship programme has commenced 200 students were registered for the exam out of which more than 60 students are awarded with scholarship. In 2013, 8.5 thousands students were applied for AISEE exam and more than 2000 students got scholarship for there engineering studies. In 2014, 18 thousand candidates submitted the exam application form and 8 thousand Scholarship for Medical Students has been provided. In 2015, 40 thousand students were registered for the exam out of which more than 16 thousand students are awarded with scholarship. In 2016, 80 thousands students were applied for AISEE exam and more than 20 thousand students got scholarship for there engineering studies. In 2017, 1.8 lakhs candidates submitted the exam application form and 28 thousand Scholarship for Medical Students has been provided. Last year 2.1 lakh students attended the entrance exam and more than 37 thousand candidates claimed the scholarship amount by clearing the All India Scholarship Entrance Exam.

Selection Criteria For AISEE 2019

It is very important that you read the AISEE eligibility criteria properly before you apply for exam.

  • Candidates who have cleared class 12th or those who are pursuing class 12th will be eligible to attend Scholarship for Medical Students entrance exam.
  • Candidates who have passed class 12th in 2016, 2017 and 2018 or appearing class 12th exam in 2019 will be eligible for AISEE 2019 examination.
  • Those who are applying for Scholarship for Medical Students examination must have Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics(for Engineering)/Biology(for Medical) in their class 12th curriculum.
Important Dates of AISEE 2019
  • Candidates can apply for the All India Scholarship Entrance Examination from 22nd October 2018 and the last date to apply for AISEE Scholarship for Medical Students exam in 26th January 2019.
  • The commencement of AISEE 2019 online examination will be on 9th May 2019 and the exam conducting process will end on 15th May 2019.
  • The AISEE 2019 Admit card will be published on 21st April 2019 and will available till the exam terminates.
  • The Scholarship for Medical Students will be provided and should be claimed from 17th September 2019 to 30th September 2019.

AWARDS AND BENEFITS OF AISEE SCHOLARSHIP

The eligible students will be awarded with the scholarships and rewards as per their performance in AISEE examination and according to the AISEE Scholarship slab.

  • AISEE Scholarships upto 100% for Engineering and Medical students of Government college.
  • AISEE Scholarships upto Rs.80,000/- for Engineering and Medical students of Private college.

AISEE 2019 Application Process

As mentioned above, the online registration for AISEE 2019 was open from 22nd of October 2018 till 26th of January 2019.

Candidates can register themselves for the Scholarship for Medical Students exam only in the online mode. The online application form will be available on our website. Before registering into AISEE 2019 exam the candidate should have a scanned copy of his/her photograph, signature and thumb impression (left hand thumb for boys and girls). These scanned copy of images should be uploaded during the submission of Scholarship for Medical Students application s form.

The image scanned copy should be colour and the candidates should not be wearing cap or goggles, but spectacles are allowed.

Candidates with unclear photograph are liable to be rejected to attend the Scholarship for Medical Students entrance exam.

AISEE 2019 Application process consist of 5 steps:
  • Register for examination
  • Filling up communication address and exam centre details
  • Pay the exam application fee.
  • Submit Academic and other details.
  • Upload the scanned copy of documents.
Stepwise Instruction on How to Apply for AISEE 2019 Scholarship for Medical Students Examination:
  • Visit the home page of AISEE or click here to apply.
  • Then select Apply Now option and click on either “AISEE 2019” or “AISEE Open Book Examination” as per your preference.
  • Select the PCB subject group, enter your date of birth, gender, name, valid mobile number and email address. Then click on “Register” button.
  • Candidates are advised to submit personal mobile number and email address as they are used to communicate will students regarding updates and other details.
  • Enter the verification code which was sent to your submitted mobile number and email address. Then click on proceed button to continue with the Scholarship for Medical Students application form submission.
  • Enter your academic details of class 10th and 12th, year of passing, parent or guardian details, annual income. Then click on “Click here to proceed” button to continue with the scholarship for engineering students application form submission.
  • Upload the scanned copy of photograph and signature in the specified format and click on proceed button to submit the AISEE 2019 application form.
  • Download or take the printout of confirmation page of the submitted application form for further reference.
  • Next, Enter your communication details and select any 4 exam center which is convenient for you. Click on “proceed” button.

AISEE 2019 Scholarship for Medical Students Entrance Exam Application Fee:

  • Regular Examination: INR 1200 for all quota (General/SC/ST/OBC)
  • Open Book Examination: INR 2000 for all quota (General/SC/ST/OBC)

AISEE 2019 Exam Pattern

  • The AISEE 2019 exam will be conducted in online mode which will be computer based test.
  • The question related to CBSE class 11th and 12th Physics, Chemistry and Biology will be asked in AISEE 2019 Scholarship for Medical Students entrance exam.
  • The question paper pattern will be multiple choice questions which consist of 4 options for each question and candidates need to select only one option as an answer.
  • The AISEE 2019 Scholarship entrance exam will be conducted for the duration of 90 minutes.
  • Candidates will gain plus four (+4) marks for every correct answer and they will lose minus one (-1) mark for every incorrect answer.
  • No marks will be added or removed if the candidate haven’t attempted the question.

Regular Exam Pattern

  • The total of 90 objective question will be asked in the AISEE Scholarship for Medical Students entrance exam.
  • The paper will be divided into 3 section (Physics, Chemistry and Biology).
  • Candidates can score maximum of 120 marks from each section and 30 questions will be asked per section.

Open Book Exam Pattern

  • The total of 120 objective question will be asked in the AISEE Scholarship for Medical Students entrance exam.
  • Candidate can score maximum of 480 marks in the AISEE 2019 regular examination.
  • The paper will be divided into 3 section (Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics(for Engineering)/Biology(for Medical)).
  • Candidates can score maximum of 160 marks from each section and 40 questions will be asked per section.

AISEE EXAM PATTERN

The exam will be conducted for 90 marks and the pattern of paper will be Multiple Choice Questions. The questions will be asked from class 12th Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics(for Engineering)/Biology(for Medical) (based on course selected).

Duration of Examination:

90 Minutes
Total Marks:

90 marks.

Question Type:

Multiple Choice Question.
Number of Questions:

90 questions and each subject, i.e Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics(for Engineering)/Biology(for Medical) carrying 30 Questions.
Marking Scheme:

1 mark will be awarded for every correct answer. There is no negative marking for wrong answers. No marks will be given for attempted questions.
