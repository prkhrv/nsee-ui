NSEE- National Scholarship Entrance Examination.

NSEE is a national level merit-based scholarship entrance exam for Engineering and Medical Studies aspirants in India.
Through its Scholarship program, NSEE has fueled dreams of more than 2 lakh talented engineering and medical
students of India since its initiation.

This program provides benefits for higher education to those students who wish to get aid in their higher studies.

The exam conducting board is registered with the Ministry of Finance act 1994 under the central excise department of India. The objective of the NSEE Scholarship program is to provide financial assistance to the meritorious engineering and medical students through the NSEE examinations.

NSEE has launched two scholarship programs-
1. NSEE 2021 Scholarship for medical and Engineering Read More
The NSEE exam will be usually conducted in the month of April and May. The exam will be conducted all across India and the examination will be conducted in Online mode (Computer Based Test, Home Based Test) only. Based on the candidate score in the NSEE examination the scholarship will be funded to the respective students.
